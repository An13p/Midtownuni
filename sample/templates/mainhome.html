WELCOME TO MIDTOWN UNI

The Midtown Uni is part of the prestigious Russell Group of universities, with outstanding facilities and the widest range of courses. We are highly respected across the globe as a centre of teaching excellence and innovative research. With 25 Nobel Prize winners among our current and former staff and students, we have a history of world firsts and brilliant discoveries, from splitting the atom to giving the world graphene. We are committed to world-class research, an outstanding learning and student experience, and social responsibility in everything we do.
